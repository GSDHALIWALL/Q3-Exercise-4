The Golden Temple

The Golden Temple was a temple made for Sikhs, though it welcomes all types of people from around the world. People with different religions are allowed to visit the temple. The Golden Temple was constructed in the 1500s and is still standing to this day. The Golden Temple has survived a war against it by the Indian Army and survived multiple natural disasters. The Golden Temple is open 24/7 and offers free food to people of any class.
